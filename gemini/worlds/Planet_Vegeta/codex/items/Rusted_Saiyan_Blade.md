
# Rusted Saiyan Blade

A standard-issue weapon for the slave class. Mass-produced, poorly maintained, but heavy enough to crack bone.

**Statistics:**
- **Type**: Melee Weapon (One-Handed)
- **Damage**: +2
- **Durability**: Medium (Hidden Quality)

**Description:**
A short, wide-bladed sword made of dark iron. The edge is chipped, and the grip is wrapped in rough leather. It hasn't been polished in a decade.
