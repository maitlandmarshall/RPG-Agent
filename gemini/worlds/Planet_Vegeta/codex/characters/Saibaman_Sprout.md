
# Saibaman Sprout

![Saibaman](Saibaman_Sprout/portrait.png)

A juvenile form of the bio-engineered Saibaman. Smaller and less durable than the mature "Green Man" variant, but just as vicious.

**Physical Description:**
A hunched, green humanoid creature about the size of a human child. Its skin is veiny and wrinkled, resembling a cabbage or exposed brain tissue. Its eyes are glowing red slits, and its claws are disproportionately large and jagged. It drips acidic saliva.

**Stats:**
- **Power Level**: 200 (Equivalent to a weak Raditz-era soldier)
- **Status**: Hungry, Feral
- **Abilities**: Acid Spit, Claw Swipe, Self-Destruct (Weak)

**Notes:**
- Grown from seeds in the arena soil.
- mindless killing machines.
