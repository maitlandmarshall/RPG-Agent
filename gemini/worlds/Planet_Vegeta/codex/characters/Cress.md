
# Cress (The Protagonist)

![Cress Portrait](Cress/portrait_v2.png)

A Slave Class Saiyan born into the dust of the Arena. Malnourished but hiding a burning potential.

**Physical Description:**

His hair is a matted, choppy mess of brown (not black) that hangs over his eyes, lacking the defiant spikes of purebred warriors.
