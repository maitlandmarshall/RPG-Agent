
# Klik (The Technician)

A Hive-Worlder alien sold into the Frieza Force. His chitinous plating gives him natural armor, and his compound eyes make him an excellent scout.

**Physical Description:**
A tall, hunched insectoid with green and brown exoskeletal plates. He communicates in a series of clicks and hisses, translated by a vocoder box around his neck. He carries a tech-harness filled with scavenged parts.

**Personality:**
Logic-driven, survival-focused. He finds Saiyan "pride" illogical and dangerous.

**Stats:**
- **Power Level**: 600
- **Specialty**: Technology / Recon / Defense
