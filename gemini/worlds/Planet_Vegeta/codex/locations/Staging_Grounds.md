
# Imperial Arena Staging Grounds

The massive industrial complex where slave warriors are processed, equipped, and marshalled before entering the main arena matches.

**Description:**
A sprawling jungle of concrete, steel, and landing pads. It sits in the shadow of the main Arena Spires.
The air is choked with smog and the noise of thousands of warriors.

**Atmosphere:**
Organized chaos. Brutal efficiency.

**Features:**
- **Processing Lines**: Where slaves are scanned and given weapons.
- **Holding Pens**: Where teams wait for their matches.
- **The Armory**: A heavily guarded bunker.
