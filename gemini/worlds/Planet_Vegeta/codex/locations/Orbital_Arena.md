
# Orbital Arena - Surface

The prelude to the main event. A massive, floating battle-station in orbit around Planet Vegeta. It acts as a filter to weed out the weak before they reach the Emperor's eyes.

**Description:**
A sprawling metallic cityscape of bunkers, landing pads, and automated turrets. It is designed to be a kill-box.
Low gravity. No atmosphere (Force Fields keep air in).

**Hazards:**
- **Incoming Pods**: Crushing damage.
- **Rival Squads**: 100 Squads total.
- **Turrets**: Automated defenses.
