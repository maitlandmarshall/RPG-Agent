# Freeza Force

![Freeza Force](Freeza_Force_sigil.png)

An interstellar military-industrial machine: soldiers, scouters, ship crews, and accountants—everybody paid in fear.

**Tags**: #faction #freeza_force #threat

## Goal
Control, acquisition, and suppression—especially anything that looks like a rebellion before it becomes one.

## Leverage
Numbers, ships, surveillance, and the promise that resistance is “bad business.”

## Current Status
“Observers” and quotas creep into local routines: inspections framed as normal procedure.
